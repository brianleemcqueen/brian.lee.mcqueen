Enter a Picture

You can submit a picture for consideration as the McQueen Tree Daily Picture.

Things that you should know about picture submission:
  >Pictures need to be in .jpg format.
  >A date stamp must be on the picture.
  >Pictures need to be e-mailed to Brian McQueen (see contact us).
  >Pictures must be received on the day it was taken.
  >The earlier the file is sent the better the chance of getting picked.

  >Your awesome picture may be beaten by a picture of my foot.
  >You shouldn't question our selection logic.
  >Complaints about why a picture was selected or not selected probably won't be answered.

  >Sending a picture to McQueen Tree gives us permission to use your picture as is.
  >Other than resizing, McQueen Tree will not alter your picture.
  >McQueen Tree will not sell your picture.
