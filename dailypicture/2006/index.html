NEW for 2006

It is easy to see that the mcqueentree daily picture has had some changes in 2006.  Right out of the gate, you will see the daily picture web page has been converted from the black background to the mcqueentree format.

BONUS PICS will show up from time to time.  The first bonus pic is on January 06.  Keep a close eye, and you may find more.  The bonus pic does not have the same requirements as the official daily picture.  You will notice on January 06 picture, there is not a date stamp.  Don't worry about it.  The bonus pic is allowed a little more freedom...

Since we have a new year, there are now year links for the daily picture year.  You will see each year has a home page that shows the first and most recent picture.
